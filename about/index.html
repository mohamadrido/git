Hi，欢迎来访。

Hi there, I’m just another goddamn IT guy.

This is my personal blog, mainly to share and record how I solve programming and life problems. All blog posts are written in Chinese; if you are not from a Chinese-speaking region, but want to know what I wrote, please use Google Translate.

In the beginning, I designed and built this Jekyll website to meet my needs. Later, to make some contribution to the open source community, I split the template and released the source code in the fall of 2019. If you like it, feel free to fork it on GitHub!
